Services

Skilled Nursing Services

Nurses combine the science of nursing with the art caring, Nurses are qualified but not restricted to provide the following services:

  • • Injections
  • • IV fluid and antibiotic therapy
  • • Parenteral nutrition
  • • Wound care
  • • Foley care
  • • Assesstment teaching an reassurance
  • • Post surgical care
  • • Care for chronic medical conditions
  • • Ostomy care and instructions
  • • Medication management

Home Health Aide Services

Certified aides avaliable to patients for assistance with personal care, any hygiene needs, light, homemaking services as well as safety and infection control.

Physical Therapy

Care provided by registered physical therapists with the aim to ease pain and restore loss of function, with a focus on range of motion and strengthening through theraputic exercise, which include:

  • • Assessment and reassessment
  • • Therapeutic exercise
  • • Gait training
  • • Patient/caregiver teaching
  • • Monitoring safety and infection control.

Speech Language Pathologist

Assist patients with speech.language disorders, which may have been impaired following illness or accident, regain communication skills or learn alternative means of expression to effectively relay their needs and feelings.

Social Services

Provide patient and family with the support needed to cope with the personal and emotional impact of illness or disability as well as locate financial and community resources.

About Us

Our Background

St. John Home Health is a homehealth care agency owned, managed and operated by nurses, first responders and a US Army veteran. The agency was founded in honor of a brother, a colleague, and a friend who served during the Covid pandemic who unfortunately didn't survive. We intend to provide you with qualit selfless care to emulate honor and remember "John Doble" for being a good and caring nurse to his patients, responsive to their needs and concerns.

Our goal is to provide skilled services to seniors and veterans in the comforts of their home keeping them healthy and safe.

We value the trust and confidence of our clients who recommend us to their friends and family.

We are committed to keep our patients healthy and safe to enhance their quality of life. We provide skilled nursing physical therapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy, social workers and home health aides to keep you healthy and safe. We have dedicated our services to benefit the Las Vegas and Nevada senior community.
